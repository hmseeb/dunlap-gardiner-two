Who We Are

A Law Firm Founded on Principle

Dunlap Gardiner, LLP was established in 2009 by Wesley C. Dunlap and Brian M. Gardiner, two friends who met at Georgia State University College of Law. They opened the firm with a clear intent: to help businesses and individuals throughout the metro Atlanta area navigate a wide range of legal challenges.

From its founding in Douglasville, Georgia, the firm has grown steadily. By 2014, growth necessitated a move to Hiram to better serve clients, and a second office in McDonough was opened to extend that reach throughout Henry County and beyond.

Today, the firm employs five attorneys and a support staff of twenty — all united by the same founding principle that clients deserve knowledgeable, honest, and diligent legal representation.
