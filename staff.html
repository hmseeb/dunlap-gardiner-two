The Team

Our Attorneys & Staff

Five attorneys and a dedicated support team of twenty — committed to providing quality legal representation across Georgia.

Founding Partners

Wesley C. Dunlap

Founding Partner

Wesley Dunlap co-founded the firm with the intent of providing accessible, quality legal counsel to businesses and individuals throughout metro Atlanta. During college, Wes operated his own lawn maintenance company — an experience that instilled a lasting appreciation for the challenges business owners face.

Before practicing law, Wes worked at an auto finance company, giving him deep insight into the finance industry he now represents. He graduated Magna Cum Laude from Georgia State University College of Law in 2007.

Education: Kennesaw State University (Business); Georgia State University College of Law, J.D. 2007 (Magna Cum Laude) Practice Areas: Finance company representation, HOA law, auto dealership representation, civil litigation Licensed: Georgia Supreme Court, Court of Appeals, Superior & Lower Courts, Northern/Middle/Southern Districts

Brian M. Gardiner

Founding Partner

Brian Gardiner brings a rich background in public service and community engagement to his legal practice. He previously served as a Victim Witness Coordinator for the Clayton County District Attorney's office and worked in employee relations for the Georgia Department of Human Resources.

Brian is a recognized authority in community association law, having authored pending legislation addressing HOA common areas and election procedures. He was honored with the CALI Award for Academic Excellence in Litigation and has served as lead counsel in over 4,000 cases.

Education: Clayton State University, B.S. 2003 (Cum Laude); Georgia State College of Law, J.D. 2007 Practice Areas: Community associations, covenant enforcement, assessment collection, lending, dealerships Notable: Authored SB 46 (2017), signed by Governor Deal; CALI Award for Academic Excellence in Litigation
