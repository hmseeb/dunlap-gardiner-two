Built on Experience

Georgia's Trusted Law Firm for Businesses & Communities

Dunlap Gardiner, LLP was established by two friends from Georgia State University College of Law — Wesley C. Dunlap and Brian M. Gardiner — with a shared vision: to provide accessible, effective legal counsel to businesses and individuals across metro Atlanta.

Since opening our doors in 2009 in Douglasville, we've grown to a team of five attorneys and a support staff of twenty. In 2014, we relocated to Hiram to accommodate our growth, and we've since opened an additional office in McDonough, Georgia.

Our Philosophy

Our Focus Is Our Clients

At Dunlap Gardiner, LLP, we know that our clients' success is our success. We take pride in working with our clients to develop and implement strategic legal solutions to ensure the continued success of your business or personal life.

As a smaller, more focused law firm, we enjoy close working relationships with each of our clients, allowing us to give every case the meticulous attention it deserves. We understand that each case involves important issues that impact our clients deeply.
