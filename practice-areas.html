Homeowner Association Law

Georgia's trusted HOA counsel

Primary Practice Area
HOA Representation

Comprehensive Homeowner Association Counsel

Dunlap Gardiner, LLP is a recognized authority in Georgia community association law. Our attorneys represent homeowner associations, condominium associations, and property management companies throughout the state on all matters arising from their operations.

Brian Gardiner is particularly noted for his HOA expertise — he authored pending legislation (SB 46, signed by Governor Deal in 2017) addressing abandoned HOA common areas and election procedures.

Our firm is also a proud member of the Community Association Institute (CAI), participating in annual conferences and staying current on the latest developments in association law.

  • Covenant drafting and review
  • Bylaw drafting and amendments
  • Delinquent dues collection
  • Assessment enforcement
  • Rules and regulations drafting
  • Election dispute resolution
  • Violation enforcement
  • Fining authority guidance
  • Common area legal issues
  • Board member representation
  • Management company support
  • Litigation representation

Collections & Civil Litigation

Protecting creditor rights statewide

Core Service
Collections

Accounts Receivable Recovery & Civil Litigation

Dunlap Gardiner represents creditors in collection matters and general civil litigation across Georgia courts. Our attorneys have extensive experience pursuing debt recovery efficiently and effectively, minimizing the time and cost involved in recovering what our clients are owed.

We represent clients in state court, superior court, magistrate court, and federal court, and our attorneys are licensed to practice throughout all districts of Georgia's federal court system.

  • Debt collection
  • Accounts receivable recovery
  • Judgment enforcement
  • FDCPA defense
  • Settlement negotiations
  • Trial representation
  • Property management collections
  • Contract disputes

Creditor Bankruptcy Law

Protecting your interests in bankruptcy proceedings

Specialized Practice
Creditor Bankruptcy

Protecting Creditor Rights in Bankruptcy Proceedings

When debtors file for bankruptcy protection, their creditors need experienced representation to ensure their interests are protected. Dunlap Gardiner represents creditors — including businesses, HOAs, finance companies, and banks — throughout bankruptcy proceedings in Georgia's federal courts.

Our attorneys are members of the Creditor's Rights Section of the State Bar of Georgia, reflecting our commitment and expertise in this specialized area of law.

  • Proof of claims
  • Objections to discharge
  • Objections to exemptions
  • Writs of possession
  • Turnover orders
  • Reaffirmation agreements
  • Lift of automatic stay motions
  • Plan objections

Finance & Banking Law

Representing lenders and dealers throughout Georgia

Core Service
Finance Representation

Legal Services for Finance Companies, Banks & Auto Dealers

Wesley Dunlap's pre-law career in the auto finance industry gives the firm unmatched insight into the needs of financial institutions and dealerships. Dunlap Gardiner represents banks, finance companies, and auto dealers across Georgia on a wide range of legal matters.

Our finance clients trust us to handle everything from routine collections to complex litigation, FDCPA defense, and regulatory compliance questions — providing the kind of reliable, efficient legal support that keeps their businesses running smoothly.

  • Auto dealer representation
  • Finance company collections
  • Bank litigation support
  • Default and repossession matters
  • FDCPA defense and compliance
  • Deficiency balance claims
  • Regulatory compliance guidance
  • Payment default disputes
